Chocolate Cake

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix dry ingredients.
  3. Bake for 30 minutes.

Pancakes

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Heat a pan and cook pancakes for 2 minutes each side.

Bolani

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Filling (leek or mashed potatoes)
  1. Make dough and let it rest.
  2. Prepare filling.
  3. Fill the dough and fry until golden.
